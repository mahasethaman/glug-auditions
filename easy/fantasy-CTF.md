## Fantasy CTF

**Category:** General Skills 
**Difficulty:** Easy  

### Problem Summary
The challenge presented a fantasy-themed environment where the objective was to explore the given information carefully and identify clues that lead to the flag.

### Approach
Instead of jumping straight to tools, I first read the problem description carefully to understand what was being hinted at. The challenge relied more on observation and logical thinking rather than heavy technical exploitation.

I analyzed the provided text and clues step by step and followed the trail left by the challenge. By connecting the hints correctly, I was able to locate the hidden information required to obtain the flag.

### Tools Used
- Logical analysis
- Basic Linux commands (when needed)

### Key Learning
This challenge showed that not all CTF problems require complex tools. Sometimes, carefully reading the problem and thinking logically is enough to reach the solution.
